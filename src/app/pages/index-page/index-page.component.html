Collaborative Analysis

Federated and differentially private implementations of the mostly used, state-of-the-art time-to-event analysis algorithms allow you to perform collaborative studies. Create projects, invite collaborators and make use of an immense amount of data without collecting it on a central place. And the best: your data stays locally at your site.

Federated Learning

Federated Learning allows an analysis on geographically distributed data. Instead of raw data, only model parameters or local statistics are shared. Partea aggregates these local statistics to a global model which is similar to the model trained on centralized data.

Differential Privacy

The resulting survival curves can reveal sensitive information about your patients. To prevent this, Partea uses differential privacy, which adds small amounts of noise to the shared statistics, such that the final result does not reveal any patient sensitive information.

Secure Aggregation

As even local statistics can in some cases reveal sensitive patient information, Partea uses additive secret sharing, hiding the actual local statistics. Only the global aggregation is then visible for your collaborators and our servers.

Survival regression with Cox proportional hazards model

Partea enables collaborative survival regressions to find biomarkers related to the survival of patients.

Survival Function, hazard ratio & logrank test

Partea offers the estimation of survival curves and hazard ratios based on the Kaplan-Meier and Nelson-Aalen estimators. Also, it allows you to compare different groups with the Logrank-test.
